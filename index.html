Qiang Hu

Assistant Researcher

Shanghai Jiao Tong University


I am currently an Assistant Researcher working with Prof. Wenjun Zhang at Cooperative Medianet Innovation Center, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Before that, I was an Assistant Researcher working with Prof. Jingyi Yu at ShanghaiTech University from 2021 to 2023. I was a Postdoc Researcher at ShanghaiTech University from 2019 to 2021. I received a Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019 and a B. Eng. degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2013. My research interests focus on 2D/3D video compression, neural rendering, and generative intelligence media.
Looking for self-motivated students (PhD, Master and Undergraduate RA) working with me. For prospective students, please send your resume and transcript to my email.

Projects

Real-Time Interactive Free-Viewpoint Video Streaming

We have developed an integrated solution that delivers live and on-demand FVV streaming services in real-time. Our solution provides an immersive and interactive viewing experience for FVV content, even on lightweight devices via mobile networks. Furthermore, our system is optimized to support UHD FVV applications on such devices, offering users an unparalleled viewing experience.

Online Experience Video

Distribute the Power of Radiance Rendering via the Cloud

We adopt a streaming-based solution where computationally intensive workloads are conducted on cloud servers, and their results are sent to the client as a video stream. It enables an immersive and interactive viewing experience for the neural radiance field on lightweight devices (e.g., cell phones, VR headsets).
